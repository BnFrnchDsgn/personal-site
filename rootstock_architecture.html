Summary
My responsibilities involved being the Design Lead for this project, overseeing the design deliverables and direction to ensure it met the high standards of the established Rootstock Architecture brand. These responsibilities included creating the new website design, which reflected a detailed understanding of both the Rootstock brand identity and architectural community in Alberta.
What I Did
I conducted the design research into ways we could capture the Rootstock brand across their website design, then used those insights to create the final design for the website. These activities involved collaborating with the Rootstock leadership to ensure my recommendations aligned with their brand goals & expectations.
Reflection
The overall project was an incredible creative opportunity to develop a design that captured a brand in a way that hadn't been done before. This would not have been possible without the input from the Rootstock team, who brought critical insights from their own design philosophy that could then be included into the actual final website design.
Programs Used
The programs used to fulfill the deliverables in this project included:
  • Google Workspace for handling project notes and communication
  • Adobe Illustrator when creating new graphic design assets for the website
  • Adobe XD to create the design for the new website
  • Adobe Photoshop when optimizing client provided project renders and photography
